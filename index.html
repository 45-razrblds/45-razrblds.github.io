1. MacBook Air (M1, 2020)

  • Processor: Apple M1 chip with 8-core CPU and 8-core GPU
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Storage: 1 TB SSD
  • Display: 13.3-inch Retina display with a resolution of 2560 x 1600 pixels
  • Battery Life: Up to 18 hours of wireless web usage
  • Operating System: macOS

2. iPhone 12 Pro

  • Processor: Apple A14 Bionic chip with 6-core CPU and 4-core GPU
  • Memory: 6 GB RAM
  • Storage Options: 128 GB, 256 GB, or 512 GB
  • Display: 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display with a resolution of 2532 x 1170 pixels
  • Camera System: Triple 12 MP rear cameras (ultra-wide, wide, telephoto) and a 12 MP front-facing camera
  • Battery Life: Up to 17 hours of talk time
  • Operating System: iOS

3. iPad (10th Generation, 2022)

  • Processor: Apple A14 Bionic chip with 6-core CPU and 4-core GPU
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Storage Options: 64 GB or 256 GB
  • Display: 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display with a resolution of 2360 x 1640 pixels
  • Camera System: 12 MP rear camera and 12 MP front-facing camera
  • Battery Life: Up to 10 hours of surfing the web on Wi-Fi or watching video
  • Operating System: iPadOS
